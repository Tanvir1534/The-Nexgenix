Carnival

A carnival is a lively, colorful celebration filled with music, dance, and vibrant costumes. Often marked by parades, games, and rides, it brings together people of all ages to enjoy festive foods, dazzling lights, and exciting attractions. Carnivals celebrate community spirit and cultural traditions, creating an atmosphere of joy and excitement for everyone involved.
